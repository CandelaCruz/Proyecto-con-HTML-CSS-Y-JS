A poterry studio dedicated to crativity and comunity

Sarah Glass ceramic is a women-owned poterry studio located in Fort Lauderdale. Our mission is to provide everyone of all skill levels a strong foundation and supportive enviroment to experiente the relaxing art of pottery making. We arehappy provide studio space and equipement to let you channel your inner artist.

With over 15 years of experience on the pottery wheel, Sarah Glass is a skilled ceramic artist and dedicated instructor. She has taught pottery for over five years and built a loyal community of students and clients throughout the tri-county area. Known for her delicate, thin-walled pottery and innovative glazes, Sarah creates modern stoneware and porcelain collections that bring artistry and elegance into everyday spaces.
